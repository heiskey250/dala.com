Everything we do

Our Services

Custom Design

We bring your vision to life with custom colors, patterns, and sizes. Every detail is handcrafted to match exactly what you imagine.

Worldwide Shipping

From Kigali to the world, we deliver with care and love. Every package is wrapped beautifully and tracked every step of the way.

Repair Services

Handmade quality we stand behind. We offer professional repairs for all our bags to keep your piece looking beautiful for years.

Gift Wrapping

Make any occasion special with our luxury gift wrapping service. Perfect for weddings, birthdays, and celebrations of all kinds.

Wholesale Orders

Looking to stock our pieces in your boutique? We offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders with a minimum of 10 pieces per design.

Beading Workshops

Learn the art of beading directly from our craftswomen. We host in-person and virtual workshops to share our cultural heritage.
